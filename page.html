DON'T DELAY YOUR TREATMENT
Risks of Delayed Treatment
  • Minor Problems become Major Problem
  • Recovery time increases
  • Quality of life Declines
  • Compensatory/ New problems Develop
    • Benefits of Treatment
  • Return to physical Activity
  • Rejoin family activities
  • Reduction in Pain
  • Quality of life improves
    • For individuals with an ACL tear the chance of a meniscal tear increases by 2% with each of delay !.
